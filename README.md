# End-to-End Bike Sales Analytics Dashboard

End-to-end sales analytics project built in Microsoft Excel to analyze customer demographics, purchasing behavior, income levels, education, and regional trends. The project transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights through interactive dashboarding and KPI-driven analysis.

## Executive Summary

Understanding customer purchasing behavior is critical for improving sales performance and identifying high-value customer segments.

This project analyzes bike sales data to uncover demographic and behavioral patterns influencing purchase decisions. Using Microsoft Excel, interactive dashboards were developed to provide business stakeholders with insights into customer income, age groups, education levels, commute distances, and regional performance.

---

## Business Problem

Businesses need to understand which customer characteristics are most strongly associated with purchasing behavior in order to optimize marketing campaigns and improve sales performance.

This project addresses key business questions such as:

- Which customer groups are most likely to purchase bikes?
- How does income influence purchasing behavior?
- Which age groups generate the highest sales?
- How does commute distance impact purchasing decisions?
- Are there regional differences in customer behavior?

## Dashboard Features

## Key Insights

- Higher-income customers demonstrated stronger purchase tendencies.
- Middle-aged customers represented the largest buyer segment.
- Commute distance showed a measurable relationship with purchasing behavior.
- Customer demographics played a significant role in sales outcomes.
- Regional differences influenced overall purchasing patterns.
